TEDxADIS Muroor Youth

TEDxADIS Muroor Youth is a dynamic platform where young minds converge to ignite change. This inspiring event brings together visionary speakers, thought-provoking ideas, and passionate youth to explore the endless possibilities of tomorrow. Join us as we embark on a journey of innovation, empowerment, and transformation. Be a part of the conversation that shapes the future! This independent TEDx event is operated under license from TED.

What is TEDx?

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxADIS Muroor Youth, where x = independently organized TED event. At our TEDx​ADIS Muroor Youth event, TED Talks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.

History

Our journey began on October 15, 2017 with the inaugural TEDxYouth@ADIS event, where we first embarked on a mission to provide a platform for young thinkers and doers to share their ideas and inspire change.

Following the resounding success of our first edition, we returned even stronger on October 7, 2018, for our second edition. It was a day filled with insightful talks, engaging discussions, and the celebration of youthful creativity.

Today, we proudly present the third edition of TEDxADIS Muroor Youth, building on the foundation of our past experiences and pushing the boundaries of inspiration further than ever before. As we continue to evolve and innovate, we invite you to be a part of this exciting journey towards a brighter and more dynamic future. Welcome to TEDxADIS Muroor Youth 2023!

Frequently asked questions

Your Questions, Answered: Everything You Need to Know About TEDxADIS Muroor Youth

What is TEDxADIS Muroor Youth?

TEDxADIS Muroor Youth is an independently organized TEDx event focused on empowering and inspiring youth through TED-style talks, discussions, and activities. It provides a platform for young speakers and innovators to share their ideas and experiences.

When and where will the event take place?

TEDxADIS Muroor Youth 2023 will take place on October 26, 2023, at the Sheikh Zayed Auditorium in Abu Dhabi, UAE.

How can I attend the event?

Attendance at TEDxADIS Muroor Youth 2023 is by invitation only.
