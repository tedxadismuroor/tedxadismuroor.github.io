Event Details
TEDxADIS Muroor Youth

TEDxADIS Muroor Youth is a dynamic platform where young minds converge to ignite change. This inspiring event brings together visionary speakers, thought-provoking ideas, and passionate youth to explore the endless possibilities of tomorrow. Join us as we embark on a journey of innovation, empowerment, and transformation. Be a part of the conversation that shapes the future!

History

TEDxADIS Muroor Youth has a rich legacy of empowering young voices and fostering innovation. Our journey began with the inaugural TEDxYouth@ADIS event on October 15, 2017, where we first embarked on a mission to provide a platform for young thinkers and doers to share their ideas and inspire change.

Following the resounding success of our first edition, we returned even stronger on October 7, 2018, for our second edition. It was a day filled with insightful talks, engaging discussions, and the celebration of youthful creativity.

Today, we proudly present the third edition of TEDxADIS Muroor Youth, building on the foundation of our past experiences and pushing the boundaries of inspiration further than ever before. As we continue to evolve and innovate, we invite you to be a part of this exciting journey towards a brighter and more dynamic future. Welcome to TEDxADIS Muroor Youth 2023!

Frequently asked questions

Your Questions, Answered: Everything You Need to Know About TEDxADIS Muroor Youth

What is TEDxADIS Muroor Youth?

TEDxADIS Muroor Youth is an independently organized TEDx event focused on empowering and inspiring youth through TED-style talks, discussions, and activities. It provides a platform for young speakers and innovators to share their ideas and experiences.

When and where will the event take place?

TEDxADIS Muroor Youth 2023 will take place on October 26, 2023, at the Sheikh Zayed Auditorium in Abu Dhabi, UAE.

How can I attend the event?

Attendance at TEDxADIS Muroor Youth 2023 is by invitation only.
